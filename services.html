Primary Care Services

At Rosewell Healthcare, we provide comprehensive primary care services to help you maintain your overall health and well-being. Whether you need routine check-ups, preventive care, chronic disease management, or treatment for common illnesses, our dedicated healthcare professionals are here to support you. We focus on personalized, patient-centered care to ensure you receive the best treatment for your unique needs.

Our primary care services include:

  • Annual wellness exams & preventive screenings
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, etc.)
  • Acute illness treatment (cold, flu, infections)
  • Immunizations & vaccinations
  • Women's health services
  • Medication management

We are committed to providing high-quality, compassionate care to every patient. Schedule an appointment today.

Mental Health Services

Your mental well-being is just as important as your physical health. Our mental health services provide compassionate support and evidence-based treatments to help you navigate life's challenges. Whether you're experiencing stress, anxiety, depression, or other mental health concerns, we are here to help with personalized care.

Our mental health services include:

  • Anxiety & depression management
  • Stress & burnout support
  • Therapy & counseling referrals
  • Medication management for mental health conditions
  • Holistic and integrative approaches to well-being

At Rosewell Healthcare, we believe in treating the whole person -- mind and body.
